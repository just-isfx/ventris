Warning Signs You’ve Been Scammed

  • Sudden disappearance of your crypto
  • Someone contacted you first promising “guaranteed returns”
  • Pressure to act fast or send more money
  • Fake customer support or recovery “experts” asking for fees upfront
  • Wallet drained after clicking a suspicious link

If you see ANY of these signs → Act NOW!
